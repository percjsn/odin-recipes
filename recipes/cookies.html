Cookies

Homepage

A cookie (American English), or a biscuit (British English), is a baked or cooked snack or dessert that is typically small, flat and sweet. It usually contains flour, sugar, egg, and some type of oil, fat, or butter. It may include other ingredients such as raisins, oats, chocolate chips, nuts, etc.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Baking Soda
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate Chips

Directions

  1. Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla.

  2. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture.

  3. Stir in flour and chocolate chips.

  4. Drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet.

  5. Bake until the edges are golden brown.
